Nextcloud -by RPA4All is the official Android client for secure access to the RPA4All corporate environment at https://nextcloud.rpa4all.com.

With the app you can:
* sign in with your RPA4All corporate account
* access, upload, move and share files securely
* keep important folders available offline
* sync content between your phone and the corporate environment
* enable automatic photo and video upload
* receive activity and update notifications
* protect access with PIN or biometric unlock

Important:
* this app is preconfigured for the RPA4All environment
* an organization-managed account is required
